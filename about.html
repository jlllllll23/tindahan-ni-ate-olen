About Us

Your trusted neighborhood store for over 12 years

Who We Are

Tindahan ni Ate Olen has been the go-to neighborhood store in Bagong Pagasa since 2013. We take pride in providing affordable groceries, fresh produce, and everyday essentials to the local community. Our friendly service and quality products have made us a trusted name in town.

Our Mission

We aim to deliver high-quality products at wallet-friendly prices. Our mission is to strengthen community ties and bring convenience to every household by offering essentials that matter most.

Why Choose Us?

  • Affordable everyday prices
  • Fresh and quality products
  • Friendly and helpful staff
  • Convenient location in your neighborhood
